﻿About the Job
As part of the Talent Acquisition product dev team, you will build features for our AI-powered talent product. You will be responsible for medium to large projects, owning technical design and execution. You will collaborate closely product managers, designers, and your tech lead to deliver these features.

The Opportunity
We are seeking a talented and passionate Software Engineer to join our team, focusing on building and enhancing AI-powered web applications. In this role, you will be instrumental in bringing our advanced AI models to life through intuitive and powerful user interfaces. You will work across the full stack, contributing to both the frontend and backend of our core products, ensuring a seamless and intelligent user experience for our enterprise customers.

What You'll Do
Design, develop, and deploy scalable and robust web applications that leverage our AI and machine learning capabilities.
Collaborate closely with AI/ML engineers, product managers, and UX/UI designers to translate complex AI functionalities into user-friendly features.
Contribute to all layers of the application stack, from database design and API development to responsive frontend interfaces.
Optimize web applications for performance, scalability, and security, ensuring a smooth experience for thousands of users.
Participate in code reviews, technical discussions, and architectural planning to uphold high standards of software quality and innovation.
Identify and resolve technical challenges, debug issues in cloud environments, and continuously improve our development processes.
Stay up-to-date with emerging technologies and industry best practices in AI, web development, and cloud computing.
What You Bring
Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field is preferred
[Minimum 3-5 years] of hands-on experience in full-stack web development, with a strong understanding of both frontend and backend technologies.
Proficiency in at least one modern frontend framework (e.g., React, Vue.js) and experience with JavaScript/TypeScript.
Experience with backend development using languages such as Python (preferably Flask), Java, Node.js, or similar.
Familiarity with database technologies (SQL, NoSQL) and experience with designing and interacting with APIs (RESTful, GraphQL).
Understanding of cloud platforms (e.g., AWS, GCP, Azure) and experience with deploying and managing applications in a cloud environment.
Demonstrated passion for artificial intelligence and machine learning, with an interest in building AI-driven products. 
Familiarity and passion for AI tools like Cursor, Claud, Gemini, etc in software development skills.
Strong problem-solving skills, attention to detail, and a commitment to delivering high-quality software.
Excellent communication and collaboration skills, with the ability to work effectively in a fast-paced, agile environment.
